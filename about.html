My Story

Growing up in Kenya's vibrant culture, I was surrounded by bold patterns, vivid colors, and a spirit of innovation. This environment shaped my creative perspective in ways I couldn't have imagined.

My journey into tech began when I discovered I could combine my love for art with problem-solving through design and code. What started as curiosity became an obsession - staying up late to master new techniques, often with nothing but YouTube tutorials and determination as my guides.

Today, I channel that same passion into every project at Elite Design SV, blending cultural inspiration with technical precision to create work that's both beautiful and functional.

"Design is not just what it looks like, it's how it works and makes people feel"
